Name: Graham Adkins

Background
  • Date of Birth: 10/23/1990
  • Gender: Male
  • Location: Los Angeles, California
  • Work place: Alt Marketing, Marketing Coordinator
  • School: Fairfax High School
  • Technology Level: Intermediate
Main Points

Graham is a 32-year old man, working in marketing. His tasks at work are usually quite stressful, since he has to coordinate an entire team of employees.

Goals

Personal Goals: He wants to relax watching a comedy movie after a long and exhausting day at work.

Frustrations and Pain Points

Lately, he has realized that he has seen almost every comedy movie. He became tired of searching for movies, so he has started browsing for sites that recommend movies.

Detailed Description

"A persona is a user archetype you can use to help guide decisions about product features, navigation, interactions, and even visual design." (Kim Goodwin, Cooper.com)


He is frustrated by the fact that the other employees make his job harder than it should actually be. So, at the end of the day, the only things that make him happy are his dogs and a good, relaxing movie on his laptop.


He does this every night. Eat popcorn, cuddle with the dog on the sofa and watch a good movie. He has seen so many movies that he doesn't know what to watch anymore.

Scenarios

One day, he came home after work stressed because one of the employees he coordinates erased all of the data and information that Graham and his team worked on that day. So, he had to redo all of his work the next day. Just the thought of having to deal with all of those tasks once again made him anxious and angry. But he couldn't do anything about it except accepting the situation and relax. The deed was done. So, he played a bit with Tarzan, his dog, and later he searched a comedy movie on this site, MoviePicker. He felt way better after that and the next day at work he was more relaxed.
